INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the Thohoyandou Community Gardens Project. Our mission is simple: to fight food insecurity through the power of community and sustainable agriculture. Located in the Tshidimbini village, we are a collective of local residents, farmers, and volunteers working to ensure every household has access to fresh, nutritious food.
